The Web Triad

Web development isn't just one language; it's three distinct layers working in harmony. Think of them as concentric circles.

  • Outer: Structure (The Foundation)
  • Middle: Presentation (The Look)
  • Center: Behavior (The Action)

The "Why" in Coding Decisions
